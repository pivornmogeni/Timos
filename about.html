About TIMO'S

Discover the story behind Eldoret's premier beauty destination

Our Story

Founded with a passion for beauty and excellence, TIMO'S Makeup & Nails Spa has been transforming lives through premium beauty services in Eldoret. What started as a dream to create a sanctuary where elegance meets expertise has blossomed into the city's most trusted beauty destination.

Located in the heart of Eldoret at Safaricom House, we've built our reputation on delivering exceptional service, using only the finest products, and creating an atmosphere where every client feels pampered and valued.

Our journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and radiant. Today, we continue to honor that belief through every service we provide.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional beauty and wellness services that enhance natural beauty while creating a luxurious, relaxing experience for every client. We strive to be Eldoret's premier destination for beauty transformation.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading beauty and wellness spa in Kenya, setting the standard for excellence in service, innovation, and client satisfaction while empowering individuals to embrace their unique beauty.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We never compromise on quality. Every service is delivered with meticulous attention to detail and the highest professional standards.

Care

Our clients are family. We provide personalized attention and create a warm, welcoming environment for everyone who walks through our doors.

Innovation

We stay ahead of beauty trends and continuously invest in the latest techniques and products to offer cutting-edge services.

Integrity

Honest communication, transparent pricing, and ethical practices form the foundation of our business relationships.
