Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us at +254 714 109550, visiting our spa directly, or using our online booking form on the website.

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday through Sunday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We're here to serve you 7 days a week!

Do you accept walk-ins?

While we accept walk-ins based on availability, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get your preferred time slot.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, mobile money (M-Pesa), and bank cards for your convenience.

Do you offer group bookings?

Yes! We offer special group packages for bridal parties, events, and celebrations. Contact us to discuss your needs.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Just bring yourself! We provide all necessary materials and products. For nail services, please remove any existing polish beforehand.
